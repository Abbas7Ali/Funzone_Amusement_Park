CAREER

Love having fun?

We at Fun Zone serve from the heart with energy, passion and fun -to provide memorable experiences for all our guests! Bringing to life our STAR values (Service,Tearnwork, Acting with integrity, Results-oriented), we are empowered to make a difference and make things happen.
Here at Fun Zone, we provide a fun and stimulating work environment, and an opportunity to be part of a growing organisation. Whether it is working full-time, part-time, on contract or on internship, we invite you to dive into this fun-making journey with us today.
We also believe in developing young minds and giving students opportunities for exposure. From corporate projects to events and frontline experiences, our interns are expected to get hands on during their stint with us.
If you are currently an undergraduate or a polytechnic student and would like to take up an internship with us, please drop us a note to express your interest. You will then be on a journey will) us to create and deliver the One-Fun Zone Experience through outstanding service and world class offerings. You may contact us at internships@funzone.gov.sg

In the State of FUN, play is never out of the equation.
As makers of FUN, we are committed to helping our Funzonians make time for fun. From weekly workout sessions to recreational activities and team outings, the fun never ceases on this sunny place.
