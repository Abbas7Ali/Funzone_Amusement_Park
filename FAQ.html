FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We are happy to help. Find answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our attractions, hotels, restaurants and more in Sentosa.

Islander Membership

Are there any membership schemes in Sentosa?

Islander membership now comes absolutely FREE, with effect from 1 April 2021, for you to enjoy and discover Sentosa with the following perks:

  1. Priority access to all beaches
  2. Complimentary 2-hour parking at Beach Station
  3. Members’ Day Promotions
  4. Year-long discounts at Sentosa participating outlets
  5. Birthday Treats
  6. UNLIMITED complimentary Island Admissions via the Sentosa Express & Gantry

Visit here to find out more on the benefits and privileges.

Is there still the option of the Family Islander Membership?

W.E.F from 31 March 2020, we have ceased the sales of Family Islander Membership. You may sign up for the Complimentary Islander Membership here.

I have a child under the age of 12 years. Do I need to sign up Islander membership?

Children under 12 years old are entitled to free entry to Sentosa with their child or student Ez-link card. However, you may consider signing your children up for Islander Membership at no charge. Do note that each membership requires a unique mobile number and email address.

Attractions & Island Partners

Does my child require a ticket to visit the attractions?

Children below 3 years old enjoys complimentary admission to the island and attractions. We would also like to remind parents/guardians that some attractions have minimum height requirement. Please visit the respective attractions website for further information.

Would I be able to purchase island admission and attraction tickets online?

Yes, you may purchase Sentosa Express tickets, attraction tickets and Sentosa Fun Passes from Funzone Online Store. Alternatively, you may also buy the tickets at any Sentosa Station Ticketing Counter, located at Level 3 on your day of visit.

Is filming or taking wedding photo shoots on the island allowed?

Filming and photo shoots for personal use is allowed on the island. A permit is only required if the footage is for commercial or corporate use. Please contact us for further clarifications.

Some areas, such as Tanjong Rimau (Siloso Headland) and Mount Serapong, are also important biodiversity sites with a rich array of flora and fauna. A permit is required if you wish to conduct research studies, photography, filming or other programmes at any of the nature areas mentioned above.

Facilities

Are there any Automated Teller Machines (ATM) in Sentosa?

ATMs are located at the following locations:

- Sentosa Golf Club (DBS)

- Quayside Isle (DBS)

- Resorts World Sentosa

Would I be able to purchase island admission and attraction tickets online?

Yes, you may purchase Sentosa Express tickets, attraction tickets and Sentosa Fun Passes from Funzone Online Store. Alternatively, you may also buy the tickets at any Sentosa Station Ticketing Counter, located at Level 3 on your day of visit.

Are baby prams and/or wheelchairs available for rent in Sentosa?

Islandwide rental services for baby prams and wheelchairs are not available.
